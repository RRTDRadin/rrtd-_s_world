Arsenal



Aim Practice

Arsenal is a shooter—so aim is everything. Use flick shots and tracking to hit heads. Train daily in public matches or use aim trainers inside Roblox if available.

Weapon Familiarity

You get a different weapon with each kill. Learn how to use every weapon efficiently—shotguns, snipers, rocket launchers, and even the golden knife. Every weapon has a trick to it.

Movement and Positioning

Strafe, jump, and move unpredictably to dodge bullets. Use the map’s design—ramps, corners, and rooftops—to get better angles and surprise opponents.

Game Sense

Know where enemies spawn, where they rotate, and how to predict their movement. A good player can flank and pick off multiple opponents before being seen.

Fast Weapon Switching

Be ready to adapt quickly as your weapon changes. Don’t panic if you get a sniper in close range—learn how to quickscope or jump-shot with it under pressure.

Map Knowledge

Know the ins and outs of every map. Use this to take shortcuts, escape death, or flank around enemies. Map control = kill advantage.

Health Pack Control

After a kill, health packs drop. Picking them up during fights gives you survival chances. A real pro uses these to win 2v1s or stay in the fight longer.

Sound Awareness

Listen for footsteps, gunfire, and respawn sounds. Sound can alert you to enemy locations even before you see them.

Killstreak Management

Play smart when you're on a streak. Avoid risky duels, move carefully, and build up to the golden knife smoothly. One mistake can lose your entire lead.

Competitive Mode and Events

Join competitive servers and events to challenge skilled players. These high-pressure matches help you improve faster and expose your weaknesses so you can improve them.
