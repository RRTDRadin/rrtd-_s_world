Adopt Me



Pet Growth

Raising pets is essential. Complete pet tasks quickly—like feeding, showering, and sleeping—to level them up. A full-grown pet can be turned Neon or Mega Neon, which greatly increases their value.

Smart Trading

Understand market trends and pet values. Use trusted value lists and check if your trade is fair. Focus on trading up—offering multiple rare pets for a legendary is a smart move when done right.

Event Participation

Join every limited-time event. These often give access to exclusive pets, vehicles, or toys that later become high-value trade items. Save extras to trade when the event ends.

Bucks and Grinding

Earn in-game currency by doing jobs like pizza maker or salon stylist. Log in daily, complete tasks, and collect paychecks. Use your Bucks to buy eggs, decorate houses, or upgrade pets.

Family Roleplay

Join or create families with others to complete pet tasks faster. Roleplaying as a parent or caretaker lets you grow pets quicker while also engaging in fun, themed stories.

House Design

Build stylish or themed homes that fit your character or pet collection. Add pet care stations to make aging easier. Creative houses also attract attention during public roleplays.

Pet Tricks and Showcases

Teach your pets tricks and show them off. Take them to the School for lessons and use tricks in roleplays or videos. Players love pets that can perform actions and look unique.

Style and Dressing

Match your outfit with your pet’s theme. Use the avatar editor to create cool looks for both you and your pets—matching costumes or accessories make you stand out in crowds.

Safety in Trading

Always use the trading confirmation system. Never give away pets for promises of duplication or giveaways. Trade only in safe, public spaces or with friends.

Content Creation

Adopt Me is perfect for YouTube or TikTok roleplays. Record your pet trades, house tours, or fun skits and share them to become part of the community’s creator scene.
